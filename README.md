# AmrCash-privacy-policy
AmrCash – Privacy Policy

Last updated: [today’s date]

AmrCash does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data.

All financial information you enter (income, expenses, goals, bills) is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to it.

The app does not require an account and does not collect personal identifiers such as name, email, phone number, or location.

If future versions include ads or analytics, they will follow Google Play policies and will not access your financial data.
